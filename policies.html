Financing

Here at Durham House Pets we accept cash, credit, debit, checks, and apple pay. We have a price match policy in place as well. If you can provide proof of a lower price at any store within the U.S. then we will match the price (online excluded).

Delivery

We offer delivery on fish tanks within a 50 mile radius. Delivery is only $29.99 for fish tanks under 150 gallons, and an additional $.99 per gallon after that.

Return Policies

We have a 12 month 100% satisfaction guarantee or your money back on ALL products. Just return the product to us, and we will provide you with your money back or a store credit if requested.
